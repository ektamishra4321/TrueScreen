The guardrail, in three moves

quote

The model must show its work

Each criterion score requires 1–3 verbatim quotes copied character-for-character from the resume. No quote, no score — the model abstains instead.

verify

A checker with no ML in it

A deterministic verifier confirms every quote exists in the raw PDF text. It even recognizes two-column PDF extraction scrambling — marked separately, never silently.

stamp

Verdicts you can audit

Verified, gapped-extraction, nullified, or abstained — every criterion carries its status. The weighted total is computed in Python. The LLM does no arithmetic.
