# FP&A Analyst — Financial Planning & Analysis (2-5 years)

We are hiring an FP&A Analyst to own budgeting, forecasting, and management
reporting for a growing business.

## Responsibilities
- Build and maintain annual budgets, rolling forecasts, and variance analyses
- Prepare monthly management reporting packs (P&L, cash flow, KPIs) with commentary
- Partner with business teams on headcount, spend, and unit-economics planning
- Develop and improve financial models (revenue drivers, scenario analysis)
- Support month-end close reviews with accounting and flag anomalies

## Requirements
- 2-5 years experience in FP&A, business finance, or a Big 4 / CA background
  moving into analysis roles
- Advanced Excel modelling skills (lookups, pivot tables, scenario building);
  Google Sheets acceptable
- Strong understanding of P&L, balance sheet, and cash flow interactions
- Experience with variance analysis and communicating drivers to non-finance stakeholders
- Exposure to BI tools (Power BI / Tableau) or SQL is a plus
- CA / CFA / MBA (Finance) or a commerce degree with strong analytical track record
